Leonardo Da Vinci

Leonardo Da Vinci, known as the "Renaissance Man" due to being talented in a wide variety of ways is a highly-esteemed polymath. He was born in Italy in the year 1543. Da Vinci's discoveries and creations caused a domino effect regarding the development of Science and The Arts. Even up to this day, he is regarded as one of the best artists and is heavily looked up by the masses.
