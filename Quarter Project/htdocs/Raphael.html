Raphael

Raphael, a prominent figure during the Renaissance was not only a painter, but a sculptor, an architect, and an archeologist as well. He was born in Italy in 1483 and was known to be independent during his youth. He finished his first commission known as "Madonna and Child" when he was only 15 years of age! At 17 years old, he was considered a fully trained "master". This is more evidence to why Raphael is one of the most respected artists.
