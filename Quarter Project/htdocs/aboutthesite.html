About the Site

Site Description

ATTA is a website that features a few of my favorite artists and their most well-known masterpieces. Going through the site will be like a travel through time because each piece of art could be associated to a particular time frame. The goal of this is to introduce and educate people not interested with art, to the classics, and to hopefully raise interest among the visitors of my site. Ever since my youth, I've always loved art, and I wish to ignite the passion of many to art through this website.
